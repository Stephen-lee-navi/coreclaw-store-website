Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Google Maps scraper? ▼

A Google Maps scraper is a tool that automatically extracts business data from Google Maps, including names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, emails, ratings, reviews, and more.

Unlike manual copying—where you'd spend hours clicking through listings and typing information into a spreadsheet—a scraper can extract hundreds or thousands of records in minutes.

CafeScraper goes beyond basic scraping by:

  • Verifying email addresses for deliverability
  • Validating and formatting phone numbers
  • Enriching data with additional signals
  • Providing clean, structured exports ready for your CRM or outreach tools
Is scraping Google Maps legal? ▼

Yes, scraping publicly available business data from Google Maps is generally legal in most jurisdictions. Business listings on Google Maps are considered publicly accessible information.

However, it's important to:

  • Use the data responsibly and ethically
  • Comply with data protection regulations like GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (California)
  • Respect website terms of service and robots.txt directives
  • Avoid scraping personal data from individuals without consent

CafeScraper helps you stay compliant by:

  • Providing built-in GDPR and CCPA compliance tools
  • Offering data retention controls
  • Supporting right-to-deletion requests
  • Providing transparent data usage guidelines
Can I extract email addresses from Google Maps? ▼

Yes, CafeScraper can extract email addresses from Google Maps business listings when they're available.

How it works:

  1. We scan the business listing for email addresses
  2. We cross-reference the business website to find additional emails
  3. We verify each email for deliverability
  4. We provide a validity status (valid, invalid, unknown) for each email

Why this matters: Many scrapers simply extract emails without verification, leading to high bounce rates and wasted outreach efforts. CafeScraper's email verification ensures you get deliverable addresses, improving your contact rates by up to 3x compared to unverified lists.

Note: Not all Google Maps listings include email addresses. Availability varies by business type, region, and individual business preferences.
