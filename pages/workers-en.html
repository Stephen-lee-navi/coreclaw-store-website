Frequently asked questions

What are Workers? ▼
Workers are serverless cloud automation programs. They take standard JSON inputs to independently execute complex web scraping, data processing, or AI tasks, and return results as structured data.
How do I use CafeScraper Workers? ▼
Simply sign up for CafeScraper account, then choose a ready-made solution from the Store that fits your business needs (such as Amazon, Google Search, or YouTube). Set your input, click "Start," and wait for your data to arrive.
What data formats do Workers support? ▼
Workers support multiple formats to ensure a seamless fit with your workflow:
JSON: Supports complex nested objects, preserving the logical hierarchy of the original webpage.
CSV: Automatically converts scraping results into clean, row-and-column spreadsheets.
Do I need to manage servers myself? ▼
No. The CafeScraper platform handles all the underlying infrastructure for you, including server management, smart proxy rotation to bypass anti-bot protections, and secure data storage.
Can developers earn money from the Workers they build? ▼
Yes. Developers can not only host their code on CafeScraper for personal use, but also publish it to the CafeScraper Store and set their own pricing. When other users run your Workers, you earn a recurring revenue share.
