Is it legal to scrape Google Maps? ▼

Yes, extracting publicly available business data from Google Maps is generally legal in most jurisdictions. The data displayed on Google Maps (business names, addresses, phone numbers, hours, etc.) is publicly accessible information.

However, it's important to use the data responsibly, comply with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA), respect website terms of service, and avoid using data for spam or unsolicited marketing.
